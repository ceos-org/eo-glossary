# Accuracy

## 1 Definition

Proximity of Measurement results to the accepted Value; precision is the degree to which repeated or reproducible measurements under unchanged conditions show the same results.

### Notes
- In essence, Accuracy reflects how close measurements are to the accepted Value, while precision gauges the reliability and consistency of those measurements.
