# Policy Milestone

## 1 Definition

Markers of significant progress in addressing specific issues, or crucial decision points where significant choices are made. Concrete examples include: policy planning and proposing, impact assessment, implementation, or implementation cycles (e.g., Water Framework Directive, Marine Strategy Framework Directive), achievement of Policy Objectives and Policy Targets, and evaluating and improving existing laws.
