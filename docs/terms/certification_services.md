# Certification Services

## 1 Definition

A NCEI director certification of archive authenticity.

### Notes

NCEI certifies hardcopy data products (generally PDF) that are generated both offline and online. Customers pay for certification from the NOAA E-Commerce system and Pay.gov. The products are typically used in litigation and insurance claims.
