# Contact

The primary channel for contributions, questions, bug reports, and feedback is **GitHub**:

- [Open an issue](https://github.com/ceos-org/eo-glossary/issues) or [start a discussion](https://github.com/ceos-org/eo-glossary/discussions) on the repository

For general inquiries related to CEOS WGISS:

- **CEOS WGISS** — [wgiss.ceos@gmail.com](mailto:wgiss.ceos@gmail.com)

For policy-level or institutional inquiries:

- **EC Knowledge Centre on Earth Observation (KCEO)** — [EC-KCEO@ec.europa.eu](mailto:EC-KCEO@ec.europa.eu)
- [https://knowledge4policy.ec.europa.eu/earthobservation_en](https://knowledge4policy.ec.europa.eu/earthobservation_en)
