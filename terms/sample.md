# Sample

## 1 Definition

Subset of one or more entities.

### Notes
- A Sample may be spatial, temporal, spectral, or in any other dimension or Trait.
- Each Sample is contiguous, i.e. it does not consist of more than one discontinuous geometry in any dimension.
- The Process of obtaining a Sample is called sampling.
