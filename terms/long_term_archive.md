# Long Term Archive

## 1 Definition

System for long-term storage of data, separated from primary media for compliance or archival purposes, organized as files or cataloged structures.

### Notes

A facility that provides permanent active archive functions for archived holdings during and beyond the scope of the mission or project, receiving responsibility for these holdings and providing long-term access to users. - EOSDIS
