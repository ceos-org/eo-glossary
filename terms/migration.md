# Migration

## 1 Definition

Transfer of digital information to new hardware or software environments to preserve it and overcome technological obsolescence, excluding format conversion as reprocessing.

### Notes

The transfer of digital information, while intending to preserve it, within the OAIS. It is distinguished from transfers in general by three attributes: • a focus on the preservation of the full information content; • a perspective that the new archival implementation of the information is a replacement for the old; and an understanding that full control and responsibility over all aspects of the transfer resides with the OAIS.
