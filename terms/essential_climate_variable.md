# Essential Climate Variable

**Synonyms**: ECV, Essential Climate Variables

## 1 Definition

Physical, chemical, or biological variable or group of linked variables critical to characterizing Earth’s climate and providing empirical evidence for climate understanding, prediction, and service provision.
