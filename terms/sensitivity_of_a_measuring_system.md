# Sensitivity of a Measuring System

## 1 Definition

Quotient of the change in an indication of a measuring system and the corresponding change in a value of a quantity being measured.
Note: sensitivity can depend on the value of the quantity being measured. The change considered in a value of a quantity being measured must be large compared with the resolution.
