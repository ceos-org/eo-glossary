# Area of Interest

## 1 Definition

Zone in 2D or 3D for which the Information or Data is requested. It can be discontinuous in space — in other words it can consist of the union of many separate zones (e.g., Natural Reserves in Africa, Urban environments, etc.). The attribute indicates a request for spatially complete Data within the AOI zone(s).
