# Data Quality

## 1 Definition

Metric—such as a number, graph, uncertainty budget, or flag—that enables users to evaluate the fitness for purpose of data or derived products.

### Notes

Quality Information: Secondary data required to assess the primary data set’s fitness for purpose, e.g. calibration and validation data and quality control results.
